This game, called "Cute Robots", was created in 72 hours by a team of only myself. It was made in the PICO-8 fantasy console as a submission to the Ludum Dare 43 game jam. The Ludum Dare 43 game jam's theme was "Sacrifices must be made". The game can be played here, as well as on itch.io



----
The controls are:
Arrow Keys to move
Z to Confirm/Open Menu
X to Close
