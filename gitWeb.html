Aren Taylan - Gameplay Programmer



This game, called "Cute Robots", was created in 72 hours by myself. It was coded in Lua with the PICO-8 fantasy console as submission to the Ludum Dare 43 game jam. The Ludum Dare 43 game jam's theme was "Sacrifices must be made." The game can be played here, as well as on itch.io



----
The controls are:
Arrow Keys to move
Z to Confirm/Open Menu
X to Close
----
OBJECTIVE:
Your goal is to make robots to defeat a big alien monster.
The catch? You have to sacrifice small, cute robots to do it!
Open the menu and navigate to robots to select them. After selecting them,
hit “send” to send them to go gather resources. Eventually they will return but
unable to work any more. At that point, you can kill them for extra resources to make
better robots.
