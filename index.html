A Sinking Feeling

Made in Unity. Ludum Dare 48 submission which placed in the Top 5% of games submitted for the event. Created in 72 hours with a small team of 4 people, overcoming a language barrier and utilizing unique methods of communication.

Sinking Feeling is a score attack platformer where you play as three characters controlling several different mechanisms within a submarine. The goal is maintain the health of the submarine for as long as you can, as it gradually becomes harder and harder to do so.

You can play the game in browser on itch.io

Ship Runner

Made in Unreal Engine 4. Utilizies performance improvement technqiues such as object pooling to avoid eccessive creation of objects. Currently still in development.

Ship Runner is an infinite runner arcade shooter where you play as a ship fighting an endless onslaught of enemy ships. You can only fire in the direction your ship is moving, so aim carefully.

You can download this game on itch.io

Of Sun and Moon

Made in Godot Engine. Developed alongside another developer for the GMTK 2021 Game Jam on a team of 7 total people. Responsbile for the main mechanics and menu navigation.

Of Sun and Moon is a "shoot-em-up" style bullet hell game with a set of 3 levels. Unlike other bullet hell games, your only weapon is a detaching flail which is tethered to you and controlled by your movement.

You can play this game in browser on itch.io

ROMP

Made in Unity. Submission for Texas A&M's 2019 Chillenium Game Jam. Developed in 48 hours, coded in Unity alongside another developer. Responsible for functionality such as custom camera behaviour and multiplayer gameplay mecahnics.

Romp is a multiplayer party game where you and a friend compete to collect food.

You can download this game on itch.io

Cute Robots

Made in PICO-8. Ludum Dare 43 submission which was made entirely inside of the fantasy console, PICO-8. Developed in 48 hours, coding in the Lua language.

Cute Robots is a simple and short resource management game where you create robots to collect materials and and reuse the old husks of used robots to create even more.

You can play this game in browser on itch.io OR here!
